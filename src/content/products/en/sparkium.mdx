Sparkium is a modern standard designed to revolutionize the eLearning industry. The goal is to enable creators, publishers, organizations and system administrators to create, manage and distribute eLearning materials in an efficient and where possible, automated manner.

## How does Sparkium work?

By using Sparkium's approach of a structured file system and text-based format you achieve more accessible, portable, interoperable and future-proof eLearning materials, while also supporting your workflow of change management and version control.

1. Create and modify eLearning materials: Use Sparkium's structured format to create and edit your eLearning materials.
1. Synchronize with Learning Management Systems (LMS): Use Sparklink to validate and upload your content to LMS platforms like Moodle with ease.

## Why to use Sparkium?

Sparkium understands your pain points, and it is built to fix them.

### Edit with just a text editor

- Got an text editor? That's all you need to edit your eLearning content!
- No technical barriers. Anyone in your team can jump in and contribute!
- No more unreadable LMS export files! Sparkium keeps everything in clean, easy-to-read text formats.

### Update once. Use everywhere

- Need to update the same content in multiple places? Stop copy-pasting - Sparkium allows you to edit once, reuse it across courses.
- Online or offline, in your LMS or locally - your workflow stays the same!
- Got a preferred way to track changes? Use it. Sparkium works with your workflow.

### Never get lost in your course structure again

- Ever got lost in your own LMS? Sparkium turns your course structure into clear file systems.
- No hidden logic, no guessing, just clear and straightforward organization.
- Find the file you need in seconds - not after endless clicking.

### Not locked in. Fully portable.

- Free your content from being tied to any single LMS platform.
- Export, modify, and reuse your content across systems with minimal effort.
- Maintain full control of your content - access or move it whenever you need.

## Sparkium, what else?

eLearning standards today vary in flexibility, complexity, and how easy they are to work with.  
Here's how Sparkium compares to existing approaches:

| Comparison                     | SCORM                              | xAPI                                       | Sparkium                                                                       |
| ------------------------------ | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Ease of Content Creation**   | 🟡 Requires LMS setup              | 🟡 More flexible but needs technical setup | 🟢 Create in text-based format                            |
| **Update & Change Management** | 🔴 Hard to update existing courses | 🟡 Can track some changes                  | 🟢 Content can be updated locally and re-uploaded seamlessly                   |
| **Structured Organization**    | 🔴 File structure not standardized | 🟡 Partially structured                    | 🟢 Clear, logical file system makes content easy to find and maintain |
| **Content Portability**        | 🔴 Tied to LMS                     | 🟡 Can move between xAPI-enabled platforms | 🟢 Export, modify, and re-upload within supported LMS                          |
| **Technical Barrier**          | 🔴 Technical skills required       | 🔴 Technical skills required               | 🟢 No specialized tools or technical skills required                                   |

Most eLearning content today is created directly inside LMS platforms.  
Sparkium takes a different approach.

| Comparison              | LMS-native content                          | Sparkium                                                                 |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Where content lives** | Inside the LMS only                         | Stored as structured files outside the LMS                              |
| **Editing experience**  | Done in LMS editors                         | Editable as plain text in any text-editing tool                                      |
| **Updating content**    | Manual edits inside each course             | Update once and reuse across courses                                    |
| **Content reuse**       | Limited and often duplicated                | Modular and reusable by design                                          |
| **Portability**         | Hard to export or migrate                   | Easy to export, modify, and re-upload                                   |
| **Transparency**        | Hidden behind LMS interfaces                | Fully visible file structure                                            |
| **Ownership**           | Controlled by the LMS                       | Fully owned and controlled by you                                       |
