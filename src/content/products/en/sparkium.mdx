Sparkium is a modern standard designed to revolutionize the eLearning industry. The goal is to enable creators, publishers, organizations and system administrators to create, manage and distribute eLearning materials in an efficient and where possible, automated manner.

## How does Sparkium work?

By using Sparkium's approach of a structured file system and text-based format you achieve more accessible, portable, interoperable and future-proof eLearning materials, while also supporting your workflow of change management and version control.

1. Create and modify eLearning materials: Use Sparkium's structured format to create and edit your eLearning materials.
1. Synchronize with Learning Management Systems (LMS): Use Sparklink to validate and upload your content to LMS platforms like Moodle with ease.

## Who is Sparkium for?

Sparkium is made for anyone working with eLearning content:

### Individuals

- **Indie-Educators** \
Do you want your eLearning materials to be accessible to more learners? Sparkium allows you to create eLearning content locally, import and publish them to modern LMS (e.g., Moodle) - start with just a text editor, no platform lock-in, no technical barriers.
- **LMS Administrators** \
Isn't it difficult to manage eLearning materials when different formats keep creating extra work? Sparkium standardize eLearning materials into one format, and eliminates the chaos of managing incompatible files.

### Organizations

- **Publishers** \
Do you miss the days when schools bought textbooks directly from you? Sparkium helps you bring that model into the digital era. Create eLearning materials once and deliver them across multiple LMS platforms, including Moodle, so your content can be sold and launched easily on customers' systems.
- **Schools** \
Tired of constantly editing content inside your LMS? Sparkium removes platform friction from your workflow. You can update the eLearning materials locally, and deploy them when ready.
- **Universities** \
Seeing good resources but cannot easily add them into your course in LMS? Sparkium allows you to compose eLearning materials from multiple resources, and keep them fully compatible with your LMS.
- **Training Centers** \
Do you need to develop courses faster while customizing for different needs? Sparkium allows you to reuse and customize existing courses quickly to deliver tailored courses for different clients and use cases.
- **Internal Training Teams** \
Do you wish to onboard new employees and train teams more efficiently? Sparkium helps corporate trainers to create, adapt, and reuse eLearning materials, even across teams and roles, making internal training easier to scale.

If this sounds like you, Sparkium is built for you!

## Why to use Sparkium?

Sparkium is designed to tackle your pain points of building eLearning materials.

### Edit with just a text editor

- Got a text editor? That's all you need to edit your eLearning materials! Your content stays in clean, easy-to-read text formats, making it simple to edit and maintain.

### Interoperability

- Tired of copy-pasting the same content into multiple courses? Sparkium makes it possible to edit once and reuse your eLearning materials across different courses and learning environments.

### Simple Change Management

- Difficult to track changes? Sparkium offers the possibility to cooperate your eLearning materials with version control tools (e.g., Git). Allowing you to track your changes in a mannered way.

### Fully portable

- Do you want full control over your content instead of only being allowed to edit via LMS? Sparkium allows you export from LMS, modify on your local device, and reuse your eLearning materials again with minimal effort.

### Zero Lock-in

- Is your eLearning material trapped inside LMS? Sparkium frees your eLearning materials from platform dependence, so you can use them on an LMS or move them to other compatible systems whenever needed.

### Quality Assurance

- Is it difficult to review content quality when your materials are hard to read or edit? Sparkium keeps your eLearning materials in a clear format, making it easy for you and your team to check, fix, and improve quality.

### Automation Possibility

- Do you want to automate parts of your content workflow? Sparkium works well with tools like GitHub Actions, so you can integrate your eLearning materials into automated workflows.

### Easy Internationalization

- Do you need to create eLearning materials in multiple languages? As Sparkium stores your content locally in an editable format, you can duplicate your files, translate and adapt them more easily for different languages.

| Comparison              | LMS-native content                          | Sparkium                                                                 |
