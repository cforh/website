Sparkium is a modern standard designed to revolutionize the eLearning industry. The goal is to enable creators, publishers, organizations and system administrators to create, manage and distribute eLearning materials in an efficient and where possible, automated manner.

## How does Sparkium work?

By using Sparkium's approach of a structured file system and text-based format you achieve more accessible, portable, interoperable and future-proof eLearning materials, while also supporting your workflow of change management and version control.

1. Create and modify eLearning materials: Use Sparkium's structured format to create and edit your eLearning materials.
1. Synchronize with Learning Management Systems (LMS): Use Sparklink to validate and upload your content to LMS platforms like Moodle with ease.

## Who is Sparkium for?

Sparkium is made for anyone working with eLearning content:

### Individuals

- **Indie-Educators** \
Do you want your eLearning materials to reach more students without being locked into one platform? Sparkium uses a standardized format that makes it easy to upload your eLearning materials to popular LMS platforms like Moodle, so your content can be accessed by more learners.
- **LMS Administrators** \
Is it difficult to manage eLearning materials when different formats keep creating extra work? Sparkium simplifies change management by keeping all eLearning materials in a consistent format, making LMS administration easier and more efficient.

### Organizations

- **Publishers** \
Do you miss the days when schools bought textbooks directly from you? Sparkium helps you bring that model into the digital era. Create eLearning materials once and deliver them across multiple LMS platforms, including Moodle, so your content can be sold and launched easily on customers' systems.
- **Schools** \
Tired of constantly editing content inside your LMS? Sparkium removes platform friction from your workflow. You can update your eLearning materials locally at any time and publish them whenever you're ready.
- **Universities** \
Isn't it annoying when you see good sources, but cannot easily integrate into your eLearning material? Sparkium allows you to composite them into your courses easily, then to upload and display it in your LMS. Sparkium also allows your eLearning material to be collaboratively and sustainable.
- **Training Centers** \
Do you need to develop courses faster while meeting different customer group's specific needs? Sparkium helps you quickly customize eLearning materials for different departments and clients base on your previous eLearning material, while turning your courses into scalable and reusable products.
- **Internal Training Teams** \
Do you need to onboard new employees and train teams efficiently, while keeping content up to date? Sparkium helps corporate learning teams create, adapt, and reuse training materials for different roles and departments. It reduces content duplication, simplifies updates, and makes internal training easier to scale.

If this sounds like you, Sparkium is built for you!

## Why to use Sparkium?

Sparkium is designed to tackle your pain points of building eLearning materials.

### Edit with just a text editor

- Got a text editor? That's all you need to edit your eLearning materials! Your content stays in clean, easy-to-read text formats, making it simple to edit and maintain.

### Update once. Use everywhere

- Tired of copy-pasting the same content into multiple courses? Sparkium lets you edit once and reuse your eLearning materials across different courses and learning environments.

### Simple Change Management

- Got a preferred way to track changes? Use it. Sparkium allows you to view the old changes and adapt new changes easily.

### Fully portable

- Do you want full control over your content instead of being stuck in one system? Sparkium lets you export, modify, and reuse your eLearning materials with minimal effort.

### Zero Lock-in

- Is your content trapped inside LMS? Sparkium frees your eLearning materials from platform dependence, so you can use them on an LMS or move them to other systems whenever needed.

### Quality Assurance

- Is it difficult to review content quality when your materials are hard to read or edit? Sparkium keeps your eLearning materials in a clear format, making it easier for you and your team to check, fix, and improve them.

### Automation Possibility

- Do you want to automate parts of your content workflow? Sparkium works well with tools like GitHub Actions, so you can integrate your eLearning materials into automated workflows.

### Easy Internationalization

- Do you need to create eLearning materials in multiple languages? Because Sparkium stores your content locally in an editable format, you can duplicate it, translate it, and adapt it more easily for different languages.

| Comparison              | LMS-native content                          | Sparkium                                                                 |
