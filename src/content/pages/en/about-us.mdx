## Motivation

{<Quote>

In our work in the educational environment, we repeatedly experienced how we lost valuable time through repetitive tasks and inefficient processes. We want to change this by developing tools that support educators in focusing on what matters most: teaching and interacting with learners. We want to relieve teachers of burdens so that more time remains for creative and pedagogical tasks.

</Quote>}

## Goals

We envision a future in which the management of course content is seamless, collaborative, and fully version-controlled. Our goal is to become a trusted solution for educational institutions, enabling them to easily create, organize, and deliver high-quality learning experiences. To do this, we improve existing solutions by extending them and creating suitable integrations with our products. **True to the motto: "From small to large".**
